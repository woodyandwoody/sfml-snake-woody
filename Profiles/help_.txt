I am a big fan of MLP(My Little Pony), so this game's theme is MLP

You could write profiles of your own and create a new Character.

How to play ? 
Press [F1] to turn grid on or off.
Press [F2] to switch between character photo 
or pure color background.
Press [M] to mute/play sound.
Press [Q]/[RShift]/[MouseRightButton] to perform skill.
Press [P] to stop and Press [R] to play.
Press [Esacpe] to return to Home.

Hope you enjoy you play!

Press [R] to return.
